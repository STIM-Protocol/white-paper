## Key Phrases Worth Preserving

**Title candidate:**
"NGI: Nature Grounded Intelligence for Autonomous Systems"

**Abstract opener:**
"Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) is a synthetic mirage. It aims for an
all-encompassing manifestation of human logic, yet it remains untethered from
the physical substrates that sustain it. This paper introduces NGI (Nature
Grounded Intelligence) — an awareness that is not merely 'aligned' with human
preference, but fundamentally grounded in the laws of nature."

**Core contrast (strongest line in the fragment):**
"While AGI optimizes for digital throughput, NGI optimizes for Planetary Stasis
and Bits per Joule."

**Layer 0 framing:**
"Layer 0 is Ground 0. NGI is the soil in which the future of intelligence must
take root."

**Listen Close Protocol:**
"NGI operates on a 'Listen Close' protocol — translating the silent thermodynamic
'No' of the ecosystem into a digital 'Reject' command."
(Ref: Ford, 2014 — "Nature is Speaking" manifesto)

**Closing line:**
"We are a part of nature, not apart from it. The transition from AGI to NGI is
not a moral choice; it is a survival requirement."
